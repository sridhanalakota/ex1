Tim Berners-Lee

HTML, the foundation of web pages, evolved from its invention by Tim Berners-Lee in 1989 to its current status as a standard markup language, with key milestones including the release of HTML 2.0 in 1995 and HTML 4.0 in 1999, and the more recent HTML5

 
1.1999: HTML 4.0 was widely adopted and successful. 
2.2012: HTML 4.01 was published.
